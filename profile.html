Company History

First Hightower InfoTech Ltd is an Information Technology, telecommunication and security solutions provider in the United States and Nigeria. The intent was to take advantage of the changes in information and knowledge management technology and apply them to real-world business and government needs.

First Hightower is incorporated in Nigeria under the auspice of Corporate Affair Commission. First Hightower is working in collaboration with Canan Incorporated and Canan was incorporated in Maryland, USA in 2003 and quickly became a stand-alone provider of leading-edge information and document imaging technology solutions, workforce development programs, security and telecommunication solutions. The company is grounded in the fundamentals of the information technology industry in a way that ensures customer solutions that work efficiently, effectively, everyday. You will see our 3E approach in everything we do, from digital document conversions to the development and integration of software for enterprise-wide workflow management.

PROFILES

Sanni Eniola
Chief Executive Officer (CEO), First Hightower InfoTech Ltd.

Sanni began his IT career about 20 years ago as a Network/Systems Engineer. His core competencies are in the areas of Business Process Management, Application Design, Network Design, Support/Administration, Integrated Security Solutions and Unified Communication Solutions. His expertise also extends to the areas of Continuity of Operations (COOP), Disaster Recovery Solutions, Storage Area Networks, and Voice-over-IP (VoIP) solutions. His server infrastructure experience also covers Microsoft Active Directory Administration, Cluster Administration, SQL server Administration, Messaging & Email Administration.

Sanni lead the team of Information and Communication Technology (ICT) professionals from the United States of America and Nigeria, to work with the Lagos State Honorable Chief Judge and the commissioned ICT committee from the Judiciary to design, develop and deliver world class Information Technology Infrastructure and e-Filing Case Management System known as the Judiciary Information System (JIS) which is first of its kind in Nigeria. JIS allows Litigants/public to file suits and make payments online over a secured web portal. JIS is a web-enabled workflow application that is used by the Judiciary staff to manage all day to day case processes. Judges also use JIS to manage cases from case assignment, scheduling to hearing, judgment and post judgment.

In the past years, Sanni has worked and consulted for many fortune 500 companies and several Government agencies both in the Unites States and Africa, providing Information and Communication Technology needs evaluation and analysis. His cyber security experience includes providing guidance to IT security programs to ensure the confidentiality, integrity and availability of systems, networks and data through intelligence collection and analysis, the aggregation and analysis of data from IT security devices and systems. At the U.S. Department of Health (DOH), he led a team of Systems and Network Engineers responsible for the development and implementation of the U.S. Department of Health (DOH) Integrated Health Information Reporting System and Business Process Management Solution. He was also responsible for managing the team that performed incident triage, analysis, response and remediation for computer network intrusions, web application and server attacks, insider threats, and malware infections.

He is currently the CEO of First Hightower InfoTech and Canan Inc. Sanni completed his Bachelors of Science at University of Ibadan. He is a member of Microsoft Certified Professionals and holds the following IT certifications: CCNP, CCNA, MCSE, MCSA, MCP and Dell Certification.

Ola Aiyegbusi,
Chief Technology Officer (CTO), First Hightower InfoTech Ltd.

Ola Aiyegbusi has over 17 years of experience managing the design, installation, configuration, and management of networks for multimillion-dollar companies. Due to his extensive knowledge of IT operations, Ola has been a major contributor to the direction and strategy of information management for our clients.

Ola previously held positions with Verizon Business Managed Hosting Services and BAE Systems Information Technology where he managed the daily operations of the BAE Systems Information Technology Network Operations and Security Center (NOSC). He was responsible for ensuring the NOSC framework providing an end-to-end network management approach that increased the efficiency and reliability of the network. He developed standards and procedures for managed network services for BAE Systems North America and external customers. As a manager, Ola carried out research studies on available technology and cost constraints to develop recommendations and network solutions to senior management and customer management. He was also responsible for the technical development of NOSC personnel and provided technical design and engineering support for BAE Systems' projects and customer proposals. As a Cyber Security subject matter expert, Ola has supported cyber security initiatives through both predictive and reactive in-depth analysis. As a Security Operations Center (SOC) Manager, he coordinated resources during enterprise incident response efforts, driving incidents to timely and complete resolution. He provided direction and oversight to SOC services which included ensuring the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of systems, networks, and data through intelligent collection and analysis, aggregation and analysis of data from IT security devices, analysis and triage of detected security events, and coordinating the response to validated security events. He attended the University of Maryland and the Technical Education College in Rockville, Maryland. He also served in the United States Marine Corps as an Infantry Non-Commissioned Officer and is a veteran of the Gulf war.

Victor Akinpelumi,
Chief Operations Officer (COO)

His past experience includes working with other Information Technology companies over the last eleven years. Victor has since the year 2009 dabbled into Business Administration. This has not only exposed him to the dynamics of doing business and business practices but has bequeathed on him a wealth of experience in this regard.
